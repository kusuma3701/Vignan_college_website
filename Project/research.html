Research Details!!

A sound scientific temperament and quest for continous innovation drives both faculty and students to keep pursuing research activities at the institute.This infuses an investigative spirit even among under-graduate students and motivates them to take up various challenging projects.To conduct and to promote research activities, VFSTR has a Directorate of Research and Development headed by a Director who is assisted by two Assistant Directors. While one Assistant Director looks after the Ph.D. programmes and the projects funded by the external funding agencies like DST, UGC, CSIR, AICTE, ISRO etc

ACOUSTICS / VIBRATION:

An advanced acoustics and vibrations center has been created at the institute to cater to the needs of the industry and also work upon further research problems. This center is currently working with research la- boratories such as NSTL, IIT Madras etc. In addition it is also providing consultancy services to industries such as Ramco, Kirloskar etc.

RF AND MICROWAVE

A Radio Frequency (RF) and Microwave center for research has been set up at the institute with the focus of working on Antenna Design & Fabrication. This center is concerned with the analysis, design and synthesis of Antennae using micro and Nanotechnology for both search and tracking applications. In this center, design and fabrication of ANTENNA is being explored which is a challenging task, due to the optical property of metal which are significantly different from the microwave characteristics.

BIG DATA ANALYTICS, IOTE

The Big Data Analytics and IoT center has been established with the objective of working regarding secure computations in distributed programming frameworks, security best practices for non-relational data stores, secure data storage and transactions logs, end-point input validation/filtering, real-time security/compliance monitoring, scalable and compostable privacy-preserving data mining and analytics, cryptographically enforced access control and secure communication, gran- ular access control, granular audits and data provenance.
