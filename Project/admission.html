Admission Details!!

Vignan’s Institute of Information Technology (VIIT) is a private institute established in 2002. The institute is located in Vishakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. Vignan’s Institute of Information Technology offers B.Tech courses in a number of specializations. The institute also offers M.Tech, MBA and MCA programs. The annual intake for UG courses is 1140 and for M.Tech, the intake is 126 students. For MBA and MCA courses, the annual intake is 120 and 60, respectively.


1.Applications for all the courses have to be submitted online.

2.Candidates should have qualified 10+2 from a recognized board for admission to B.Tech program. Applicants who have obtained B.Tech degree can apply for M.Tech courses.

3.A graduation degree is prerequisite for admissions to MBA and MCA courses.>

4.Admissions to B.Tech and M.Tech are based on candidates’ rank in Engineering Agricultural and Medical Common Entrance Test (EAMCET) and Post Graduate Engineering Common Entrance Test (PGECET), respectively.

5.For MBA and MCA, admissions will be conducted through Integrated Common Entrance Test (ICET) conducted at state level.

Vignan’s Institute of Information Technology B.Tech Admission 2022

VIIT offers 4-year B.Tech degree courses in 7 specializations. Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics as the compulsory subjects. The selection is based on the candidates’ rank in APEAMCET. The seat allotment is made by Convener, EAMCET as per the stipulated rules of A.P State Government. Candidates will be called for counseling APEAMCET on the basis of the ranks secured by the candidates in the rank list.
